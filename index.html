Customers Enjoy

One time enrollment on GrailPay™ allows Customers to automatically configure with each GrailPay™ Retailer and receive their sign-up reward.

Once a Customer has configured with a particular Retailer, they can use their signup reward when making their first purchase online or with the GrailPay™ mobile app at the register.

Customers enjoy ongoing instant cashback of 1-2% at enrolled Grocers and 2% or more at other enrolled Retailers, plus the Retailer’s customized cumulative purchase rewards and special offers managed by the Retailer’s Dashboard.

Customers can receive mobile app notification of in-store specials when at the Retailer location.
