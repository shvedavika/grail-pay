About Us

GrailPay™ originates from the inspiration of 16-year-old William Messina to find ways that common people could benefit from greater efficiencies in the way that consumers interact with retailers.

William learned about how the costs of advertising (customer acquisition) and transaction settlement (debit and credit card processing ) impact the costs of goods which led to William creating the GrailPay™ Retailer savings that are converted to instant cashback to its users.

GrailPay™ provides the Retailer savings, which fund effective customer acquisition and retention strategies.

GrailPay™ can be deployed with as few as one retailer but is expected to have at least one national retailer featured from a few of the various retailer segments such as, grocery, department store, home repair, restaurant and travel (“Founding Retailers”).
