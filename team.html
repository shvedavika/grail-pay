GrailPay™ Team

Evan Kostorizos

CEO and President | Co-Founder

Evan is an attorney that has been involved with startups since he founded his first company in 2003. His forte is helping early stage companies transform into scalable/ fundable enterprises. Evan has helped companies grow from founders to 300 employee and from $50,000 angel stage to $57MM Series D rounds of funding, and he has prior experience with building and scaling a company in the fintech/payment space. Evan excels a turning lemons into lemonade

William Messina

Co-Founder

GrailPay™ originates from the inspiration of 16 year old founder William Messina to create ways all people can benefit from greater efficiency in the way that retailers interact with consumers. William has a passion for creating ways to better the world. He began developing the concepts for GrailPay™ at the age of fourteen after discovering the amount of money that retailers expend on customer acquisition and for payment of merchandise with credit cards. These observations led to the development of the concept of creating efficiencies for the retailer that would then fund loyalty rewards and enhance engagement between retailer and customer. William has incredible passion for GrailPay™ and knows that it will become the next generation of rewards payment.

Robert Messina

Robert has experience with numerous startups as follows: logging and wood chipping in Vermont, growing certified organic aloe-vera in Florida and Belize, credit card processing, industry specific telecommunications reselling, sales organization for nutra-ceuticals and ozone water purification systems, a new technology for diagnosing coronary heart disease and consulting for tax and asset protection strategies. Judy Shapiro

Co-Founder | Board of Advisors

Judy, as CEO and founder of engageSimply, a programmatic content marketing firm, has had a distinguished career working both the agency side (NWAyer) and client side (AT&T, Lucent Technologies, Bell Labs, Paltalk and CA). Judy became an early advocate of advertising technology and by 2011, she launched engageSimply with the mission to help advertisers identify, access and buy top quality, trending-topic programmatic ad inventory. Judy is a regular contributor and speaker at industry venues including Ad Age, 4A’s and Entrepreneur aimed at inspiring her colleagues to embrace ad tech’s potential and educating marketers about ad tech’s dark side where algorithms trump intuition and platforms overshadow people. In 2017, engageSimply was awarded “Top 10 Content Marketing Solution” from Marketing Tech Insight.

Terry Herzog

Co-Founder | Board of Advisors

Terry has been in the financial industry for 50+ years. From Wall Street to venture capital, Mr. Herzog has raised millions of dollars for businesses and controlled billions on global commodities and government securities markets. Terry became a Registered Representative in 1965 and went on to become a partner in a New York Stock Exchange brokerage firm managing sales teams as well as generating new business for the investment banking department. Later, Terry went on to start his own money management firm managing more than $12B in futures, equities and government securities markets. Mr. Herzog did a number of firsts in the financial industry including the first market maker in options on the American Stock Exchange as well as creating the first non-mortgage back-securitized debt (Carpacks). Terry also has experience with startup companies, including a merchant services company.
